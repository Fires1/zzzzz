How to fix Garry’s Mod missing textures

You see this? It sucks right? It’s an easy fix.

Garry’s Mod uses textures from major Source engine games including

  • Half Life 2 (included)
  • Half Life 2 Expansions(Ep 1, Ep 2 and Lost Cost)
  • Counter Strike: Source
  • Counter Strike: Global Offensive
  • Half Life: Source
  • Half Life Deathmatch: Source
  • Left 4 Dead 1 & 2
  • Portal 1 & 2
  • Team Fortress 2

How To Fix It

Two ways:

  1. Buy the games you need.
    Generally during major Steam Sales you can get the Valve collection for a handful of dollars
  2. Download the content (kind illegally) from online
    To do this, find a site (search garrys mod texture download addon) or use a reputable site like gmodcontent.com
    Generally you only will need Counter Strike Source content for 90% of Garry’s Mod pursuits so we’ll follow that.
    After downloading you will have .zip file, use a program of your choice to unzip it and then go to your local Garry’s Mod folder (Open Steam, right click on Garry’s Mod, click properties, go to local files, then click browse local files) once you’re in your Garry’s Mod folder, open the ‘garrysmod’ folder inside it, then go into the ‘addons’ folder
    Open your zip file and move the content folder into the addons folder.
    Restart Garry’s Mod.

Success.
